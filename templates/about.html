About Cinemate

Welcome to Cinemate, your go-to platform for discovering and organizing your favorite movies. Whether you're looking for new recommendations, tracking your watched films, or curating a personal collection, Cinemate makes it easy and seamless.

About the Developer

Hey there! I'm Aziz Ibrahim, a passionate developer with a love for movies and web development. You can check out more of my work on GitHub .

Powered by TMDb

This project is made possible by The Movie Database (TMDb), which provides rich movie data, including posters, ratings, and summaries. While we use TMDb's API, Cinemate is not officially affiliated with or endorsed by TMDb.

Features

  • Personalized Favorites: Save and manage your favorite movies.
  • Infinite Scroll Movie List: Browse an extensive collection effortlessly.
  • Detailed Movie Information: Access ratings, summaries, and posters.
  • Dynamic User Experience: Built using Django, JavaScript, and AJAX for a seamless UI.

Thank you for using Cinemate! If you have feedback or feature suggestions, feel free to reach out by visiting our contact page .
